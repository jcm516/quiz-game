GUIDE:

  1. Pick a difficulty & category
  2. You need a 70% or better to pass
  3. You can reset at any time by pressing the top left button
  4. For the regular quiz, you only have 15 seconds per question
  5. Extras
    • For extra challenge, the lightning round is 3 seconds per question
    • For extra+ challenge, hardcore has no timer but will punish if you get any question wrong
